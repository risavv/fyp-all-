Pankaj Pokhreli

Pankaj Pokhreli is a fresh face in the world of drumming, but don't let his newcomer status fool youâ€”his passion for percussion runs deep. Hailing from Tanahun, Pokhreli discovered his love for drumming at an early age and has been drumming up a storm ever since. Pokhreli approaches each lesson with infectious energy and a genuine desire to share his love for the drums with others. He believes in making learning fun and accessible for students of all ages and skill levels. He understands that every student is unique, and he tailors his approach to meet the individual needs and goals of each student.

Beyond teaching and performing, Pokhreli is on a mission to inspire others to discover the joy of drumming and the power of music to unite and uplift. He believes that music has the power to transcend language and cultural barriers, and he is committed to using his talents to spread positivity and connection wherever he goes.
