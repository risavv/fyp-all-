About HMC

Who are we and what do we offer?

  1. We are committed to enabling students of all ages and ability levels to experience the joy of music learning virtually. Since our founding in 2023, we have been committed to use technology to break down barriers to music education and cultivate a global learning community. Our goal is to provide you with effective, affordable, and personalized music lessons based on your own interests and objectives. Our knowledgeable teachers and extensive curriculum are available to support you on your musical journey, regardless of your level of experience. Here in HMC, we're a vibrant learning community brought together by a common passion for music. We work to foster an atmosphere where connections are created and creativity thrives through collaborative features, live sessions, and personalized feedback. We firmly believe that music has the capacity to uplift, inspire, and bring people together, therefore we're dedicated to enabling people from all walks of life to experience the power of music.
CEO, Founder of HMC

Nidiv Kayastha

"As a lifelong musician and educator, I founded this platform with one goal in mind: to provide aspiring musicians with the tools and resources they need to succeed.

Here at HMC, we believe that music is for everyone, therefore me and my team are committed to making quality education accessible to all. Join us on this journey of discovery, creativity, and lifelong learning. Let's make music together!"
